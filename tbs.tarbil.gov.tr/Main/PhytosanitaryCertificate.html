Phytosanitary Certificates are verified in two stages.
In Stage 1, it is verified that the Phytosanitary Certificate is issued by the National Plant Protection Organisation of Türkiye.
In the 2nd stage, if the product is fresh fruit and vegetables, the exit status from the Customs Territory of Türkiye is checked.
As a result of all these checks, detailed information about the status of the Phytosanitary Certificate to be verified is given as follows.
No such Phytosanitary Certificate has been issued by the Turkish National Plant Protection Service.
The Phytosanitary Certificate has been issued by the Türkiye National Plant Protection Service.
The Product Did Not Exited From Türkiye.
The Phytosanitary Certificate has been issued by the Türkiye National Plant Protection Service.
  • The Product Exited From Türkiye.
  • The products indicated as "Export Realised Quantity" in the table below were exported from Türkiye.
Phytosanitary Certificate and Re-Export Phytosanitary Certificates can be verified with QR code. The QR code is located in 2nd place of the Phytosanitary Certificate.The Verification Result is shown in the section above the Phytosanitary Certificate. In addition, Certificates continue to be issued with wet signature and seal.
If you require additional information or have any questions about the Phytosanitary Certificate, please don't hesitate to contact us on nppoturkiye@tarimorman.gov.tr
